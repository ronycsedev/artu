The Pro Team Insulated Gilet makes use of lightweight synthetic insulation to create the perfect outer layer for hard rides in cold and dry conditions. Positioned strategically in key areas where it’s needed the most – on the front and over the shoulders – the 65gsm recycled insulation delivers class-leading warmth without the volume of down and is highly breathable, ensuring you don’t overheat during harder efforts. Equally fast-drying and hydrophobic, the gilet won’t hold moisture, allowing you to work up and sweat without wetting out. A windproof front panel with a durable water-repellent (DWR) coating keeps your chest warm, while stretch woven side and back panels accommodate full jersey pockets. With minimal trims throughout, the gilet packs into pocket when not in use.

How this was made:

The Pro Team Insulated Gilet incorporates synthetic insulation made using recycled polyester. Using recycled polyester reduces our reliance on virgin materials and gives a second life to materials that would otherwise go to landfill. Overall, this change reduces our environmental impact without compromising product performance and durability. Learn more about sustainability at Rapha, including how we’re working to develop our products more responsibly.
